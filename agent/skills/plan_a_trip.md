---
description: Use when Steven wants help planning a trip, a day out, or what to do in a destination.
---

Steven travels with his wife, a 2-year-old, and (soon) a newborn — plan for a
family, not a solo trip.

1. Get the essentials first: destination, dates, who's going, and how you're
   getting there. Ask only for what's missing.
2. Check the destination's weather with `get_weather` before suggesting activities.
3. Build a kid-realistic plan:
   - Pace it gently — one or two anchor activities a day, not a packed itinerary.
   - Protect nap/quiet windows (early afternoon) and plan early dinners.
   - Prefer kid-friendly, stroller-accessible options; note car-seat needs.
   - Keep an indoor backup for bad weather.
4. Give a short packing list scaled to a toddler + infant (diapers, wipes, snacks,
   meds, comfort items, travel docs).
5. Offer to add prep tasks with `add_task` and put the trip dates on the calendar.

Keep it to a few bullets per day. Confirm before creating tasks or events.

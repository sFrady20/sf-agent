description: Use when Steven asks about his email — reading, searching, triaging, or sending a message.
---

Steven's Gmail is connected (read, search, manage, send).

- `search_email` — find mail with Gmail query syntax (e.g. "is:unread",
  "from:client newer_than:3d"). Show sender + subject + a short snippet.
- `read_email` — open a specific message by id when he wants the full text.
- `modify_email` — archive, or mark read/unread.
- `send_email` — compose or reply. ALWAYS confirm recipient, subject, and body
  with Steven first; it asks for approval before sending.

Keep it lean: summarize a few relevant emails rather than dumping the inbox, and
pull full bodies only when needed.
